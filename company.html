About CoreLayer AI Security

A security company built from the ground up for AI

AI is being embedded into enterprise operations faster than security can follow. System prompts go unreviewed. Models are tested functionally, never adversarially. Runtime behaviour is assumed safe. The result is an expanding attack surface that traditional security tools were never designed to address.

CoreLayer AI Security was founded to close that gap — not with another point solution, but with a full-lifecycle platform purpose-built for how AI systems actually work.

Our founders bring together enterprise cybersecurity experience, deep adversarial AI research, and a track record of critical vulnerability disclosures recognised by organisations including Google, the United Nations, and the World Health Organization. We have seen how security debt accumulates when it is added after the fact. CoreLayer was built from day one to instrument security across every phase of AI deployment — from the first system prompt to runtime inference to end-user interaction.

Headquartered in Chennai, India, CoreLayer serves enterprise customers across BFSI, healthcare, IT & ITeS, manufacturing, and the public sector — bringing world-class AI security capability built on deep research and real-world adversarial expertise.

Why CoreLayer

Built differently, from the ground up

Four pillars that separate CoreLayer from every point solution in the market.

🔄

Full Lifecycle Coverage

The only platform that instruments all five phases of the AI workflow — Build, Test, Validate, Runtime, and End User — with shared telemetry and cross-phase enforcement. A vulnerability found at Build generates a Test case. A runtime anomaly feeds back into Build scanner rules.

🧩

Customisable Security Architecture

Deploy as standalone tools per phase or as a unified platform. YAML-based policy-as-code gives teams full control over every enforcement decision. No rigid enterprise lock-in — CoreLayer adapts to your architecture, not the other way around.

☁

Multi-Deployment Model

Model-agnostic and cloud-agnostic. Works across OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, and open-source models. Deploys on-premise, hybrid, or cloud-native with sub-10ms policy evaluation and zero architecture rewrite required.

🧠

Intelligence Feedback Loop

This isn't a suite of disconnected tools. Every phase feeds intelligence to the others. The platform gets smarter from every attack — runtime anomalies become Build scanner rules, successful Striker attacks become Radar detection patterns.
